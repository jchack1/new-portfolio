Design

Logos

The turtle logo and the dots logo were created using Adobe Illustrator for my SAIT course. I have used the dots logo across this site as well as on my Jekyll site, which you may view on the "Web" section of this site.

The Calgary Zoo logos were created for a graphic design course at the Alberta University of the Arts, not actually for the zoo itself. This beginner course emphasized designing by hand rather than with computers, so this set of logos are hand-drawn with black ink. You will notice some of the other design work on this page was also hand-drawn.
